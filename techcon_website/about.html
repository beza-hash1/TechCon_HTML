Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers passionate about sharing new ideas in software engineering and emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most respected global tech events.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower innovation, connect global tech communities, and inspire the next generation of changemakers. We aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Thompson

An AI researcher and MIT professor, Dr. Thompson's keynote on ethical AI design in 2021 challenged attendees to rethink responsible tech.

James Okoro

Founder of DevNet Africa, James shared his journey scaling developer communities across Africa and his work on internet accessibility.

Sophia Lin

Lead engineer at QuantumEdge, Sophia presented groundbreaking research on quantum computing in real-world applications.
